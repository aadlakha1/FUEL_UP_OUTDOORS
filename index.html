The Problem

The problem with trail food.

You plan a full day on the trail. Then you pack granola bars, trail mix loaded with candy, and call it nutrition. By noon you're dragging.

Your body needs real protein

15g protein from grass-fed beef per serving. Not soy isolate, not whey powder — actual food your body knows how to use on the move.

Sugar crashes ruin the trail

Zero added sugar means sustained energy, not a spike and crash by the next switchback. Stay fueled without the rollercoaster.

You care what goes in your body

No preservatives, no artificial anything. A short ingredient list you can actually read. If you check labels, you'll respect this one.

It fits where you go

No refrigeration, no prep, no utensils. Tear open a bag at the summit or unwrap a stick mid-stride. Lightweight fuel that goes wherever you do.
